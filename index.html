HOBBIES

Painting and Drawing

During the pandemic, my desire to draw got even stronger. I opted to concentrate my attention on drawing because I have a lot of free time. When I see the full figure forms in these sketches and paintings that start with shabby lines, these gives me excited and want to draw more.

The majority of my subjects these days are humans, particularly anime and manga characters. I like painting still life as well, but I find them to be more restrictive. Animal figures are my favorite subject to paint since they allow me to be more creative.
